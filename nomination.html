Andrew Yang

is an American entrepreneur, philanthropist, author, and lawyer who is currently running for President of the United States in the 2020 election. Initially a corporate lawyer, Yang began working in various startups and early stage growth companies as a founder or executive from 2000 to 2009. In 2011, he founded Venture for America (VFA), a nonprofit organization focused on creating jobs in cities struggling to recover from the Great Recession.
  • Born: January 13, 1975 (age 45) Schenectady, New York, U.S.
  • Education: Brown University (BA) Columbia University (JD)
  • Occupation: Attorney entrepreneur
  • Spouse(s) Evelyn Lu (m. 2011)
  • Awards: Champions of Change (2012) Presidential Ambassadors for Global Entrepreneurship (2015)
